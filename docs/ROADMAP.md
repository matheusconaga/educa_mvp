# EducAssist Product Roadmap

Version: 1.0

Status: MVP Validation

---

# Vision

EducAssist aims to become the AI copilot for teachers.

The product will evolve from a frontend demonstration into a complete AI-powered educational platform capable of assisting teachers throughout the entire planning process.

The roadmap below defines the expected evolution of the product.

EducAssist is more than a lesson planning tool.

It is an intelligent educational assistant designed to reduce repetitive work, improve teaching productivity and help educators dedicate more time to what matters most:

**Teaching, inspiring and supporting students.**
